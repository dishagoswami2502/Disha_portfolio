My Passion — Tech for Every Child

❤️ Core Belief

Every Child Deserves to Be a Creator, Not Just a Consumer of Technology

I believe that the future belongs to those who understand how things work — not just how to use them. My deepest passion is bridging the gap between complex technology and young minds. I want every child, regardless of their background, to experience the thrill of making a robot move, writing a line of code, or solving a real-world problem with their hands.

"If a child can dream it, they should be able to build it. My mission is to hand them the tools and the courage to try."

Through my work in robotics education, I've witnessed firsthand how a single 'aha!' moment can ignite a lifelong love for science and technology. That spark — that wide-eyed wonder — is what drives everything I do.
