About Me

Engineer. Educator.
Client Leader.

My career is a deliberate evolution. I started as a pure engineer — competing in national robotics competitions, writing code for microcontrollers, designing PCBs. I fell in love with how technology actually works at its core.

Then I discovered something more powerful: I could make others fall in love with it too. That realization moved me into education — training students, designing curriculum, and running government-backed programs that put robots in classrooms across Delhi.

Today, as a Regional Manager, I sit at the intersection of technology and relationships. I work directly with corporate clients like Capgemini and IDEMIA, present to government officials, manage CSR partnerships, and lead teams that deliver robotics programs at scale.
